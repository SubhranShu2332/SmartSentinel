Overview

_____________


  • Bhubaneswar has unfortunately witnessed a high number of road accidents and fatalities in the past decade. Estimates suggest over 45,000 people lost their lives in accidents across Odisha during this period, with a significant portion likely occurring in the capital city.
  • 2022 saw 5,467 deaths in 11,663 accidents, a slight increase from 2021's 5,081 deaths in 10,983 accidents.
  • However, there was a concerning rise in the first quarter of 2023 compared to the previous year, indicating potential trends needing attention.

Cause

__________

The main reason of road accidents are not obeying the traffic rules.

Government initiatives aimed at improving road safety encounter a challenge where some vehicle users adhere to traffic rules primarily due to the fear of cops. To create a more positive impact, there's an opportunity to shift the narrative from mere compliance to fostering a sense of shared responsibility with the help of science and technology.

Our Solution

_____________________

Our solution unfolds in three seamless phases.

1. Video Evidence Submission

Empower users to contribute to safer roads by effortlessly sharing videos of traffic violations through our intuitive web application. This user-friendly platform encourages active community engagement in surveillance, transform everyday individuals into valuable contributors to the enhancement of traffic safety.

2. Advanced Processing with OpenCV and TensorFlow

Immerse yourself in a cutting-edge system that seamlessly integrates OpenCV for video processing and TensorFlow for machine learning. This dynamic duo ensures unparalleled accuracy in detecting violations and conducting comprehensive data analysis. Experience the pinnacle of technology, where precision meets innovation for a seamless and efficient solution.

3. Bounty Rewards System

Elevate your commitment to road safety and be duly recognized. Our system rewards contributing users with enticing bounties for verified video evidence. This initiative not only acknowledges individual efforts but also fosters a culture of active participation and collaboration, ultimately working together for the common goal of safer roads. Join the movement where every contribution makes a difference and every participant is a valued guardian of road safety.
